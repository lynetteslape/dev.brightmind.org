Our Supporters

Bright Mind Enrichment and Schooling is:

  • Powered almost completely by volunteers –so support to us has all the more impact toward helping educate children, adults, and communities
  • A 501(c)(3) non-profit organization
  • A transparent organization –we received Guidestar’s Bronze Star in 2019
  • Hard-charging(!) –we have an 80% success rate on seeking institutional support (along with 13% partial advances)

Thank you for supporting our mission to offer community-wellness education to the whole community
